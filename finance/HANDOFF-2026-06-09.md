# Finance Handoff - 2026-06-09

## Problem and root cause

Receipt scanning was blocked by a broad photo/video permission prompt on Android 13+.
The app uses `ActivityResultContracts.GetContent`, which grants temporary access to the
selected URI and does not require `READ_MEDIA_IMAGES` or `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`.
